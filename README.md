## 📌 What is the AI Research Agent?
Most AI models rely on training data that might be months or years old. The **AI Research Agent** is different. It acts as an autonomous digital librarian that:

1. **Understands** your complex questions.
2. **Browses** the live internet in real-time.
3. **Scrapes** information from multiple trusted websites simultaneously.
4. **Writes** a comprehensive report with citations so you know exactly where the data came from.

No more hallucinations—just verified, up-to-date information delivered in seconds.

---

## 🧠 How it Thinks (The Pipeline)
The agent follows a professional research workflow using a **LangGraph** intelligence engine:

* **Step 1: Planning** – It breaks your question into several targeted sub-queries to cover all angles.
* **Step 2: Searching** – It uses high-precision search tools to find the most relevant and high-quality web sources.
* **Step 3: Scraping** – It reads through multiple web pages at once to find the specific details you need.
* **Step 4: Synthesis** – It uses high-power Large Language Models (LLMs) to summarize everything into a clear, cited report.

---

## ✨ Key Features
* 🤖 **Autonomous Search:** No manual googling required; the agent does the legwork.
* ⚡ **Incredible Speed:** Uses parallel processing and specialized hardware for fast responses.
* 📜 **Research History:** Your past searches are saved locally, allowing you to pick up where you left off.
* ⬇️ **Easy Export:** Download your final report as a **Markdown** or **PDF** file.
* 💬 **Interactive:** Ask follow-up questions to dive deeper into any specific section of the report.
